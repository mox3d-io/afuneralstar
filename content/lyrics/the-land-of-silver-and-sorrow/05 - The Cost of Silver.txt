Dust kicks up beneath my boots
Sun burns on the tin
Men in jackets pressed and clean
Ask what side I’m in
They count favors, count the coins
Measure every man
I was not raised in this dust
Just to kiss a hand

Ay señores, miren bien
No nací para vender
Mi palabra vale más
Que su ley de papel

They build houses on the hill
Iron gates and stone
Speak of order, speak of peace
From a guarded throne
But the wind across this land
Answers to no king
And the blood inside my veins
Will not wear your ring

In this town of heat and wire
Where the whispers crawl
Some men bend to rise up higher
Some refuse it all
I have seen what silver buys
I have seen the cost
Names erased and spines undone
Souls forever lost

Ay mi tierra, dame voz
Que retumbe el son
Que no mande aquí el dinero
Más que el corazón

They invite me to their table
Pour a heavy glass
Smile thin and speak of future
Built on buried past
Say a man must choose a side
Choose the winning flame
But a fire that feeds on fear
Only brands your name

I would rather walk alone
Under burning sky
Than be seated in your shadow
Learning how to lie
I would rather earn my bread
With these calloused hands
Than be owned by velvet ropes
Or your silent plans

Ahora digan todos
Que la tierra es libre
Que la sangre no se compra
Que el orgullo vive

No se vende, no se dobla
No se calla, no
No se vende, no se dobla
Aquí mando yo

When the trumpets cut the air
Let them hear it clear
I am not your quiet man
I am standing here
When the morning floods the square
Cold and bright and wide
I remain what I have been
Name intact and pride unbroken, side by side with the open sky.
