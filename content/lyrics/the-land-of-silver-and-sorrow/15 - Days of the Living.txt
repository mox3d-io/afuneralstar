Sun goes down behind the hills

Gold fades into gray

Engines idle by the mills

Like they’re here to stay

Church bells ring the evening hour

Swallows cross the sky

When you chase life not to die

Love learns not to cry

She pours coffee by the sink

Window cracked for air

Every time the brakes will squeal

She pretends not to care

He walks in with dust on boots

Smoke upon his sleeve

Kisses her and says he’s fine

Like she would believe

In this town of steel and sand

Love is not enough

Every promise sounds so brave

Spoken soft and slow

Every road he says he’ll leave

Is one he’ll never go 

Ay corazón, no preguntes
Dónde estuvo ayer
Si la noche trae secretos
Déjalos correr

They dance slow in the kitchen light
Radio playing low
A ranchera fills the night
With a tender glow
For a moment there’s no fear
No men at the gate
Just her cheek against his chest
Trying to forget

He swears one day they’ll disappear
Buy a house by the sea
Far from rifles, far from here
Just her and he
But the hills remember names
And the road remembers faces

Money moves like silver flames
Burning down their traces

Madre mía, protégelo
Cuando salga al sol
Que regrese a mi puerta
Con su mismo corazón

In this town of steel and sand
Love is fierce and fast
Every kiss is half a prayer
Like it might be the last
When the engines start again
And the night turns thin
She stands silent at the door

And lets the darkness in...
