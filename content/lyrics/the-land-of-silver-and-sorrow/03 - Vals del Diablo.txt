Dawn arrives across the plain
Cold light on the adobe wall
Roosters cry and dogs complain
Dust hangs thick above it all

Mama folds her apron tight
Lights a candle by the door
Prays her sons come home tonight
Like they always have before

In this land of sun and sand
Where the river draws the line
Fate is written by a hand
That is not yours or mine

Ay corazón, no preguntes
Lo que ya se ve
Porque aquí la vida cambia
De la noche al amanecer

There’s a boy who tends the field
With a cap pulled low and worn
He once swore he’d never yield
Now he rides before the dawn

Engines hum along the ridge
Chrome and black against the sky
Every promise builds a bridge
Every bridge can burn and die

In this land of sun and sand
Trumpets cry in minor tones
Every deal is hand to hand
Every king sits on a throne

Santa madre, cúbrenos
Bajo tu manto fiel
Porque el hombre manda balas
Y el dinero es la ley

Saturday the plaza fills
Violins begin to sing
Boots strike hard on broken tiles
Gold rings flash in candle ring

Tequila warms the trembling hand
Laughter rings but never long
Love is brave but seldom planned
Hope is fragile as a song

In this land of sun and sand
We endure what we must bear
Some grow rich and take command
Some just vanish into air

Mi amor, si no regreso
Guarda fuerte mi querer
Si la luna oye mi nombre
Déjala también saber

Night descends in velvet blue
Stars like nails in heaven’s dome
Some are feared and some are true
All just want to make it home

Dawn returns across the plain
Same dust on the same old wall
Life is loss and love and gain
And the earth will take us all
