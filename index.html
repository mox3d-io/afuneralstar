About

A Funeral Star

Experimental death metal from Washington D.C. — songs about people trapped by the gravity of their situations, like living inside a funeral star.

Our music moves from the street to the ritual to the event horizon: poverty, grief, addiction, history, collapse. Every record is another orbit around the same force.

  • Based — Washington, D.C.
  • Genre — Death · Black · Folk Metal
  • Records — Three full-lengths, an EP, a single, ongoing
